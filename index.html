THE SEALED SCROLL

a raven-post between the North and the Shire

Deep in Winterfell's rookery, the wizards of House Stark bind every message with a traveling-spell: each letter of a sentence slides forward through the alphabet by exactly as many places as there are letters in that sentence's first word. Only a reader who knows the trick — or dares to crack the wax — may deliver the words safely to Jon Snow, the magical hobbit who keeps the Wall's westernmost gate.
